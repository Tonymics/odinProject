Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice has a savory, umami-rich flavour that hints of soy sauce, garlic, and sometimes sesame oil.

The texture is slightly chewy and firm, with individual grains staying separate and lightly crisped from the stir-fry.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1/2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt & peppers to taste

Steps

  1. Prep the rice
  2. Scramble the eggs
  3. Saute the Aromatics
  4. Cook the vegetables
  5. Add rice
  6. Season, finish, serve
