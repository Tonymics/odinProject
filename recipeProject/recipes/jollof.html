Jollof Rice

Description

Nigeria Jollof has a reddish-orange color from blended tomatoes and red peppers, and gives off a savory, spicy smell with hints of herbs, onions, and tomato.

It is bold, spicy, and slightly sweet, with deep tomato and seasoning flavors.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups of parboiled rice
  • 3 tomatoes blended
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 table spoon of tomato paste
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cup of chicken stock

Steps

  1. Prepare the base ( blended tomatoes e.t.c )
  2. Parboil rice
  3. Cook the sauce
  4. Add Rice
  5. Add vegetables
  6. Garnish & Serve
